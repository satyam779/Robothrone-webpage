ü§ñ About Robothrone

Robothrone is an exciting inter-school competition designed to ignite the passion for robotics and IoT among young learners. This platform provides students from grades 3 to 9 with the opportunity to showcase their innovative ideas and technical skills.

Participants will work in teams to design, build, and program robots or IoT devices that address real-world challenges. The competition emphasizes creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration while fostering STEM education.

With expert mentorship, workshops, and resources, Robothrone aims to be more than just a competition ‚Äì it's a journey of discovery and innovation that prepares students for the technological challenges of tomorrow.
